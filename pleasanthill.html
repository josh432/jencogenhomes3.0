Pleasant Hill




Pleasant Hill, California:
Median home prices (single family home)
$830,000 in 2019
Pleasant Hill is a sunny, suburban city of approximately 34,000 residents 27 miles from San Francisco. Downtown Pleasant Hill, revitalized in the past decade or so, offers dining, walking, outdoor concerts, farmers’ markets, a movie theater, and the popular Jack’s Restaurant. Commuters enjoy a BART station and easy freeway access. This family-oriented city with many parks and beautiful open spaces boasts great schools and quality of life. Some of my favorite spots in Pleasant Hill include Melo’s Pizza and Pasta, Jack’s Restaurant, Off the Grid Food Trucks on Wednesdays, and Pleasant Hill Park.
