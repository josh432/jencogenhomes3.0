Orinda




Orinda
Median home price (for single family home) $1,500,000 in 2019.
Just through the Caldecott Tunnel from Oakland and Berkeley, Orinda appeals to families ready to leave Oakland or San Francisco for a less intensely urban environment. Just like all of the “Larmorinda” area (Lafayette, Orinda, and Moraga), Orinda boasts very highly rated schools. Its beauty, surrounded by rolling hills, culture, and its proximity to San Francisco and Bay Area activities make Orinda a delightful place in which to live, visit, and shop. The Orinda Country Club, built in 1924, is an example of beautiful classic architecture that can be found in the area. Some of my favorite spots in Orinda include the Orinda Library, the historic art deco Orinda Theater, and while I’ve never yet been, I hope to check out California Shakespeare Theater.
