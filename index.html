Hi, I'm Jen!

BUY A HOME
SELL A HOME

I know buying a home is a big deal, especially in the super competitive East Bay. Expect my unwavering devotion to helping you find your way to your next home, and me to walk you through tough decisions, problem solve, and provide consistent communication.

Selling your home, probably your largest asset, is emotional and can be stressful. I love marketing homes, thinking creatively, and leaving no detail untouched so we can maximize your sale price!

My job is to minimize pain points, educate you, and protect you during the buying or selling process. I will help you develop a strategic plan and back it up with my strong negotiation skills, unparalleled accessibility, and local market expertise.
