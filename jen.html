My job is to minimize pain points, educate you, and protect you during the buying or selling process. I will help you develop a strategic plan and back it up with my strong negotiation skills, unparalleled accessibility, and local market expertise.

I’m the daughter and granddaughter of builders, so it’s no surprise I pursued real estate. Community service is also in my family’s D.N.A. I started volunteering in high school and have never stopped-- my latest volunteer gigs are as Parents Association President, and Trustee at my kids’ schools.

Before kids, husband, and a dog, I was lucky enough to work in and travel to many parts of the world. I worked at Cisco Systems providing service, solo, to their largest corporate customer. I have an English degree from Tufts University, and a M.B.A. (in marketing and negotiation) from The American Graduate School of International Management. My background has led me to be resourceful, well educated, and adept at navigating the East Bay Market.

Thank you so much for taking the time to visit. I am thrilled to connect with you and I am excited for the journey ahead!
