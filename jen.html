A little (more) about me...


I’m the daughter and granddaughter of builders, so it’s no surprise I pursued real estate. Community service is also in my family’s D.N.A. I started volunteering in high school and have never stopped-- my latest volunteer gigs are as Parents Association President, and Trustee at my kids’ schools.

Before kids, husband, and a dog, I was lucky enough to work in and travel to many parts of the world.

If I still have your attention, here are a few things you’ll probably want to know about me: I worked at Cisco Systems providing service, solo, to their largest corporate customer. I have an English degree from Tufts University, and a M.B.A. (in marketing and negotiation) from The American Graduate School of International Management. My background has led me to be resourceful, well educated, and adept at navigating the East Bay Market.

Thank you so much for taking the time to visit. I am thrilled to connect with you and I am excited for the journey ahead!
