Lafayette




Lafayette
Median home price (single family home) $1,600,000 in 2019.
Just on the other side of the Berkeley Hills from Berkeley and Oakland, you will find the thriving, upscale community of Lafayette. Part of “Lamorinda” community (comprised of Lafayette, Orinda, and Moraga), Lafayette is known for its great schools, beautiful homes and vibrant restuarants and shopping. Lafayette, with its own BART station, is an ideal place for commuters who want to live the suburbs with highly rated school and plenty of things to do. On most days, the Lafayette Reservoir is packed with people enjoying the scenic walking trail and playground. Some of my favorite spots in Lafayette include the Lafayette-Moraga regional trail, Briones hikes, and Postino Restaurant.
